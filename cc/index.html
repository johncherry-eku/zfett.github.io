ColonelCraft is a private, survival multi-player Minecraft server for EKU students, by EKU students! We are a self-ran, non-official student group that wants to create a platform where any student can play together at any time, any day, anywhere. With our group, there is no official meeting place, no BYO-Device rules, and no set meeting times. All you need is a legal copy of Java Minecraft and an Internet connection!

ColonelCraft is currently in a beta state. As such, our server will be private and limited to a max of 12 players on the roster until we either gain an official student group status with EKU, or we gain enough donations to upgrade the server to a higher plan to allow more players to join. Until either or both of those requirements are met, ColonelCraft will remain whitelisted to a small roster of players.
