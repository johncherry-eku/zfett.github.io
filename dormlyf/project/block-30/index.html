SYNOPSIS

Show Spoilers:

You are Dan Garrett, a citizen of Block 30. Block 30 is a experimental housing project from The Combine with the goal of converting ordinary citizens into useful pawns for The Combine. You run into Sarah Levey, the organizer for The Beneath, a rebel group with the sole purpose of liberating the citizens of Block 30 and seeking justice for those murdered by Block's leadership, which includes her mother, father, and two brothers. As you climb and fight your way through the city and into the Block Leadership Sector, you find out the true intentions of The Combine for the people living within Block 30: The conversion of everyone living in Block 30 into the dream supersoldier that The Combine can use to combat the resistance fighters in City 17. What will happen when you break your way into the Block's leadership, and are you ready for what you'll find?
